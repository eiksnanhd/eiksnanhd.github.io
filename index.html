AMONG US GAMEPLAY GUIDE

Roles, Maps, Controls

ROLES:

ENGINEER
Engineers, as practitioners of engineering, are professionals who invent, design, analyze, build and test machines, complex systems, structures, gadgets and materials to fulfill functional objectives and requirements while considering the limitations imposed by practicality, regulation, safety and cost.
GUARDIAN ANGEL
The Guardian Angel gets a little bit more to do in comparison to ordinary ghosts. This role is granted to the first crewmate to be killed off, and it feels like a consolation prize. The Guardian Angel can give a living player a temporary shield to stop them from being killed.
SHAPESHIFTER
The Shapeshifter is a new and exciting role to play as. They are still an Impostor but with a new quirky ability. They can transform into any other player.
SCIENTIST
Allow the player to see all the other players vital signs, meaning they will know who is alive and who is dead from anywhere on the map. To keep that from being too powerful, the ability does have a battery that needs to be charged by completing tasks.
CREWMATE
Crewmate is the traditional main role that you'll likely be playing the majority of the time you play. You get no special powers
IMPOSTER
The Impostor is the iconic role that most people are really keen to play. There is a lot of excitement and tension that comes from playing this deceptive role.

OTHER ROLES:

DETECTIVE
The Detective can see footprints that other players leave behind.
EVIL GUESSER
The Guesser can shoot players during the meeting, by guessing its role. If the guess is wrong, the Guesser dies instead. The host can select how many players can be shot per game and if multiple players can be shot during a single meeting.
CAMOUFLAGE
Camouflager is an Impostor which can additionally activate a camouflage mode. The camouflage mode lasts for 10s and while it is active, all player names/pets/hats are hidden and all players have the same color.
BOUNTY HUNTER
The Bounty Hunter is an Impostor, that continuously get bounties (the targeted player doesn't get notified). The target of the Bounty Hunter swaps after every meeting and after a configurable amount of time.
NINJA
The Ninja is an Impostor who has the ability to kill another player all over the map. You can mark a player with your ability and by using the ability again, you jump to the position of the marked player and kill it.
CLEANER
The Cleaner is an Impostor who has the ability to clean up dead bodies. To be specific, the Clean button shares the cooldown with the kill button, to prevent the Cleaner from immediately cleaning the body of the player they have just killed.
ERASER
The Shapeshifter is a new and exciting role to play as. They are still an Impostor but with a new quirky ability. They can transform into any other player.
MEDIC
The Medic can shield (highlighted by an outline around the player) one player per game.
SHERIFF
The Sheriff has the ability to kill Impostors or Neutral roles if enabled.

MAPS

There are total of five maps:

The Skeld, Mira HQ, Polus, Airship and The Fungle

THE SKELD
Often shortened to Skeld, is the first map in Among Us set on a spaceship in outer space. It is automatically selected upon opening the game for the first time. The map was the only one included in the first version of the game's launch in 2018,
MIRA HQ
MIRA HQ is the second map in Among Us, set high up in the atmosphere of the Earth.
POLUS
Polus, also known as Polus Outpost, is the third map in Among Us, which is set on the planet Polus as a planetary base.
AIRSHIP
Often shortened as Airship, is the fourth map in Among Us, based on the Toppat Clan's Airship in the Henry Stickmin game Infiltrating the Airship. The Airship is currently the largest map in Among Us

HOW TO PLAY?

Desktop,Mobile and Console

Mouse Controls: Left-click: Move, interact, and perform tasks. Right-click: Use to report dead bodies or call emergency meetings. Keyboard Shortcuts:

Spacebar: Jump (used during certain tasks or animations). Tab: Open/close the map. E: Interact with tasks and objects. Q: Kill (only available for Impostors). R: Report a dead body. ESC: Open the menu, allowing you to exit the game, change settings, or leave a match.

Left Joystick: Move your character. Right Joystick: Navigate through menus or camera movement. A Button (or equivalent): Interact with objects, complete tasks, or confirm selections. B Button (or equivalent): Cancel or go back in menus. X Button (or equivalent): Report a dead body.

Y Button (or equivalent): Open/close the map. D-pad (if applicable): Navigate through menus or tasks. Start Button: Open the pause/menu screen. Select Button (if applicable): Access additional options.

Touch Controls: Tap: Move your character, interact with objects, and complete tasks. Tap and hold: Use to drag and move your character, especially during the map or sabotage tasks.
